The Innovations section aggregates the various databases, online dashboards, and applications built on DevInfo technology.

An easy-to-use scrolling mechanism will allow users to navigate through these various innovations. Examples include CME Info (a database containing the latest child mortality estimates based on the research of the UN Inter-agency Group for Child Mortality Estimation) and CensusInfo (a database technology for the dissemination of population and housing census results).

A short description of each innovation will be provided in this section, with the possibility of clicking on the respective icons to visit the websites (e.g. http://childmortality.org, www.censusinfo.net).
