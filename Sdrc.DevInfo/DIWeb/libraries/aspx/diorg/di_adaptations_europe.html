Bosnia and Herzegovina
DevInfo BiH  2011

DevInfo BiH is a DevInfo 6.0 adaptation containing data on key socio-economic indicators for Bosnia and Herzegovina (BiH). This pilot adaptation was developed by the BiH Agency for Statistics. Two databases are included: (1) Social Welfare 2003-2008, with data on beneficiaries, measures and services of social welfare as well as data on social welfare institutions and their employees for 2003-2008, and (2) BiH in Numbers 2010, with selected data on demographic, social and economic trends in BiH. While these pilot databases are available in Bosnian, the final database scheduled for launch in late 2011 will be available in Bosnian, Croatian, Serbian and English.

RussiaInfo  2006

Russia Info is a DevInfo 6.0 adaptation containing official statistical data on children and youth in Russia provided by the Federal Statistics Service (Rosstat). Information is sourced from two joint Rosstat - UNICEF publications, "Children in Russia 2009" and "Youth in Russia 2010," as well as from data collected on the children of Moscow, the first Russian city to join UNICEF's Child Friendly Cities (CFC) initiative. The database is available in Russian and English. Data on other CFCities of Russia are expected to become available in the future.

DevInfo Serbia is a DevInfo 6.0 adaptation that contains official statistics for monitoring the Millennium Development Goals, as well as social inclusion and poverty reduction for the Republic of Serbia.
