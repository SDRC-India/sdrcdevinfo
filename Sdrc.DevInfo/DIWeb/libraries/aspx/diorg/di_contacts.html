This adaptation is based on the DevInfo database system - an open source royalty free technology developed by UNICEF to track national and global commitments to human development. The system is easy to use and enables users to monitor progress and visualize the information through map, graph, and table objects.

The DevInfo initiative is supported by UNICEF. This technology can be adapted by a wide range of stakeholders including non-government agencies, government agencies, and inter-government agencies. Contact the persons mentioned below for more information.
