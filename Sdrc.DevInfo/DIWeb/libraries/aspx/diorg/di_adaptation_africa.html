Democratic Republic of the Congo
RDC Kasai OccidentalInfo  2010

RDC Kasai OccidentalInfo is a DevInfo 6.0 adaptation that compiles relevant human development statistics and data for the Kasai Occidental province of Congo DR. Data is sourced from surveys such as MICS, DHS, the National Survey 1-2-3 (2005), and other specific surveys (malaria, nutrition, etc.). The database also contains important routine data collected at the territorial and health zone levels in the province. The database is intended to monitor progress towards the MDGs, the Poverty Reduction Strategy, and the Provincial Action Plan and to track the situation of women and children in Kasai Occidental.
