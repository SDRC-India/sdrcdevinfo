Regional
PacificInfo  2010

PacificInfo is a DevInfo 6.0 adaptation which provides human development data for 15 Pacific Island countries through different databases. The MDG Database contains data for 15 countries: Cook Islands, Federated States of Micronesia, Fiji, Kiribati, Marshall Islands, Nauru, Niue, Palau, Papua New Guinea, Samoa, Solomon Islands, Tonga, Tuvalu, Tokelau and Vanuatu. The Pacific Subregional UNDAF Database contains data for all of the same countries minus Papua New Guinea. Databases are also available containing the UNDAF results matrices for the five least developed countries in the region: Kiribati, Samoa, Solomon Islands, Tuvalu and Vanuatu.
