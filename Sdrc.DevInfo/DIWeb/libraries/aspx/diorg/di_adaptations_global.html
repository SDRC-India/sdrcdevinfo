DevInfo 5.0  2006

DevInfo 5.0 harnesses the power of advanced information technology to provide powerful, user-friendly features to access and present data. The system is available for both desktops and web browsers. The system helps users classify indicators in many different ways and provides advanced presentation and data query tools to drill down into the data.


The system has been endorsed by the UN Development Group and is being used in many countries to help track the Millennium Development Goals and other national priorities. DevInfo 5.0 has evolved from a decade of innovations in database systems that support informed decision making and that promote the use of data to advocate for human development.
DevInfo 4.0   2004

DevInfo 4.0 is a database system that helps to organize and present data on social development indicators. Users can query data by indicators, time periods and area and then easily present the data in tables, graphs and maps. . The system provides access to indicators organized by sectors, goals, themes and other data management schemes.
Evidence to Action  2008

Evidence to Action is a DevInfo 6.0 adaptation produced by the HIV/AIDS Data Hub containing data on key HIV/AIDS indicators for 26 Asia-Pacific countries and the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region, China. It is synthesized into strategic information in five key areas: HIV prevalence and epidemiological status, vulnerability and HIV knowledge, risk behaviors, economics of AIDS and national response.
