Egypt
CensusInfo Egypt  2011

CensusInfo Egypt is a CensusInfo adaptation developed by the Central Agency for Public Mobilization and Statistics (CAPMAS) in Cairo, Egypt, with support from the United Nations Statistics Division. It contains over 90,000 data values from the 2006 census and the 1996 census. CensusInfo Egypt is available in Arabic and English languages.
