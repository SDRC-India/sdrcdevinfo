DevInfo and SDMX
 

This presentation provides an overview of how data exchange is being implemented in Cambodia using SDMX, through a DFID- and UNSD-sponsored project titled “Improving the Collation, Availability and Dissemination of National Development Indicators, including MDGs”. Through this project, data is being successfully exchanged using SDMX between line ministries and the National Statistics Office (NSO) and between the NSO and the global domain.

 di Profiles
 

Webinar on di Profiles (Bolivia context)

This webinar, delivered by Sandeep Sharma, Team Lead – Data Division, DevInfo Support Group, in October 2011 provides a general overview of the di Profile utility and its use in programme monitoring and implementation. While the webinar was targeted at implementation partners in Bolivia, the information is relevant for anyone wishing to learn more about how to create/design di Profiles and how to use them in various contexts to support monitoring activities.
