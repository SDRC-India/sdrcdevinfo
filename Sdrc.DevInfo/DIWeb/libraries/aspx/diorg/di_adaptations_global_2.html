World Economic Forum  2011

The World Economic Forum is a DevInfo 6.0 adaptation containing data published in the Global Information Technology Report (GITR) 2010-2011, considered the world’s most comprehensive and authoritative international assessment of the impact of Information Communications Technology (ICT) on the development process and the competitiveness of nations. The Networked Readiness Index (NRI), featured in the database, examines how prepared countries are to use ICT effectively in various key dimensions. This database also contains 10-year time series data on relevant Millennium Development Goals (MDG) indicators for the 133 featured countries.
