The News section allows readers to access the latest information regarding DevInfo and its adaptations: trainings, launches of new databases, and stories from its global user community (“DevInfo in Action”) in three languages. Quarterly reports will be made available, and the “Facts of the Week” section will present relevant and reliable data from trusted sources.

All stories/articles can also be downloaded in .PDF format

Managers of DevInfo adaptations can use the News section to inform their readers about any updates or relevant information.
